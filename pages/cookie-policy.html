Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how BIBlog uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Google AdSense Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. These are the main cookies used:

  • IDE — Used by Google DoubleClick to register and report user actions after viewing/clicking an ad.
  • DSID — Used to identify a signed-in user in non-Google sites to remember their preferences.
  • NID — Used to remember your preferences and other information.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at google.com/settings/ads.

How to Control Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our site.
