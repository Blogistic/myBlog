How to Use BIBlog

Everything you need to know to get started with BIBlog.

Getting Started

Create an account

Click Register in the top menu. Fill in your email and password. That's it — you're in.

Log in

Click Log In. You can tick "Remember Me" to stay logged in across sessions.

Forgot your password?

Click Forgot Password on the login page. We'll send a reset link to your email. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.
Writing & Sharing Posts

Write a new post

Tap the + button at the bottom right corner (only visible when logged in). Add a title, your story and optionally an image.

Share your post publicly

By default, posts are private (only visible to you). Hover over a post card and click the Share button to make it public.

Delete a post

Hover over any of your posts and click the Trash icon. This is permanent.
Reactions & Comments

Like a post

Tap the heart icon on any public post. Tap again to unlike. You must be logged in.

Save a post

Tap the bookmark icon to save a post. Find all saved posts in your Profile → Saved tab.

Leave a comment

Click the comment bubble icon to expand the comment section. Type your message and hit send.
Profile & People

Edit your profile

Tap the user icon FAB (bottom right) or go to My Profile. You can add a bio, profile photo, nickname and social media links.

Explore other users

Go to the Explore tab on the home page. You'll see all registered users. Click on a profile photo or username in any public post to visit their page.

Follow someone

On a user's profile page, click Follow. Their profile will then appear in your Discover tab.
Settings & Preferences

Dark Mode

Toggle dark mode from the moon icon in any topbar, or go to Settings → Appearance.

Change Language

Go to Settings → Language. Available languages: Turkish, English, German, French, Spanish, Arabic. The entire interface will update instantly.

Three-dot menu

The ••• button at the bottom right opens a quick menu with links to My Blog, Notifications and this guide.
